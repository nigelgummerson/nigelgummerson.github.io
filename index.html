Spinal Instrumentation Plan & RecordFree

Leeds Spinal Instrumentation Plan and Record

A visual surgical planning tool that replaces hand-drawn instrumentation diagrams. Documents the pre-operative plan and post-operative construct as a professional, exportable chart. Designed to run offline on hospital computers with no installation.
